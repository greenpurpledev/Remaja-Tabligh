MASJID AR RAHMAH Adalah Masjid Yang Berlokasi Di Kota Banjarmasin Utara,Masjid Ini Berada Di Kelurahan Sungai Andai


Tujuan Masjid Ini Di Bangun Adalah Agar Umat Muslim Dari Berbagai Lokasi Mau Melaksanankan Kegiatan Sholat Berjama'ah


Selain Sholat Berjama'ah,Di Sini Juga Ada Sholat Jum'at,Tahsin,Belajar Bahasa Arab,Dan Berbuka Puasa Sunnah.


Masjid Ar Rahmah Is A Masjid Located In North Banjarmasin City, This Masjid Is In If River Village


The purpose of this mosque being built is for Muslims from various locations to carry out congregational prayer activities


Apart from praying in congregation, there are also Friday prayers, tahsin, learning Arabic, and breaking the sunnah fast.
